Rachel was raised in the Blue Ridge Mountains where she learned goose wrangling and Photoshop. After watching Chasing Amy at the tender age of 14, she started making comics which helped her escape rural poverty and eventually lead to a career in web development. Now she lives with her husband in Raleigh, North Carolina, where she is training to become stronger by mastering Ruby and JavaScript.

Rachel loves studying birds and Japanese and enjoys boots, tea, orchids, making comics, and helping people learn.
